# Company Rules and Guidelines

## Working Hours
Office working hours are from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are expected to be available during these hours.

## Office Attendance
Employees should start and end their workday by marking attendance in the HR system.
Failure to do so may be considered absence.

## Work From Home
Work from home is permitted only after approval from the reporting manager.
Employees must remain reachable during official working hours.

## Holidays
Company holidays are announced at the beginning of each year.
Employees are expected to follow the official holiday calendar.

## Professional Conduct
Employees must treat colleagues with respect.
Harassment or inappropriate behavior is strictly prohibited.

## Data Security
Company data must not be shared with unauthorized individuals.
Employees are responsible for safeguarding confidential information.

## Rule Enforcement
Violation of company rules may lead to warnings or disciplinary action.
Repeated violations can result in termination.
