HR POLICY DOCUMENT

1. Paid Leaves
Employees are entitled to 12 paid leaves per calendar year.
Unused paid leaves do not carry forward to the next year unless approved by management.

2. Casual Leave
Employees may take casual leave for personal reasons with prior notice to their manager.
Approval is subject to workload and team availability.

3. Sick Leave
Employees should inform their manager as early as possible when taking sick leave.
Medical documentation may be required for extended sick leave.

4. Attendance Policy
Employees are required to mark attendance daily using the HR system.
Attendance includes start time and end time for each working day.

5. Work From Home Policy
Work from home is allowed only with prior approval from the reporting manager.
Employees must ensure availability during working hours while working remotely.

6. Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
Any misconduct may result in disciplinary action as per company rules.

7. Policy Updates
The company reserves the right to update HR policies at any time.
Employees will be notified of any changes officially.
